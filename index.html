Venue

Located in the bustling Seaport District in the heart of the city, the Seaport Hotel & World Trade Center overlooks Boston Harbor and is central to many attractions.

Program

The program committee has prepared a full and robust schedule of speakers, paper sessions, talks, and workshops the week of conference.

Workshops

Conference workshops will take place the two days prior to the main conference and will be held at Harvard University, across the river from Boston in Cambridge.

Social Events

There will be many networking opportunities and events, including the opening party at Harpoon Brewery and a conference gala at the New England Aquarium.
