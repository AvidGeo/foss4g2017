Venue

Located in Boston's Seaport Innovation District, the Seaport Hotel & World Trade Center overlooks the Boston Harbor and is central to many attractions.

Program

With a robust schedule of keynote speakers, paper sessions, and talks this event has great opportunities for learning and networking.

Workshops

Two days of conference workshops will he held prior to the main conference at Harvard University with hands on sessions for both new and experienced users.

Social Events

Many evening events are planned, including a Welcoming Party at Harpoon Brewery, an Ice Breaker at the Seaport Hotel, and a Gala at the New England Aquarium.
