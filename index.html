Welcome

The Boston Local Organizing Comittee (LOC) is proud to host the 2017 FOSS4G International Conference in August of 2017. We are currently ramping up our promotional activities and will share details of this event through this site as they become available. This launch site will be revamped into a full conference site this fall.
