Early Bird Registration is open!

The importance of registering early

The FOSS4G event is presented by the non-profit OSGeo Foundation and is organized by a volunteer Local Organizing Committee, and your registration is the largest funding source for this conference. It is vitally important to have early registration to build momentum and to properly plan for the event. Early information on how many people will be attending will help us put together the best possible conference. We have put together a very attractive early bird package that will save you money and provide other benefits.

Boston is a fabulous and busy place to visit in the Summer time. If you know you’re coming you’ll be well served by registering early and making travel and lodging reservations as early as possible. We expect that our conference hotel block at the Seaport Hotel - with a fantastic location and very competitive rate - will fill up early. Check out the details below.

The Benefits of Early Bird

Register before May 16, 2017

  • Registration price of $650, which is a $150 savings off of regular registration. Registration includes: 3 days of conference, conference meals & coffee breaks, Weds evening social, T-shirt and gift bag.
  • Workshops also have significant early bird discounts that will provide further savings
  • Free ticket to the Thursday evening Gala at the New England Aquarium, which is a $50 value. Non-early bird registrants will need to purchase a Gala ticket
  • Free ticket to the Tuesday evening “Ice Breaker” event at the Harpoon Brewery. Capacity is limited and Early Bird registrants will have priority access to the 400 tickets
  • Committing early will help ensure you get access to the workshops you want as well as the other events that will be co-located with FOSS4G on the Monday, Tuesday and Saturday surrounding the conference
  • Committing early will help you get the best rates and availability for flights and lodging, including our hotel block at the Seaport Hotel, across the street from the venue

Multiple Events Covered by Registration

During the registration process you will have the opportunity to sign up for several important events that will be happening in parallel and in Boston alongside the main FOSS4G Conference.

  • Over 40 hands-on workshops will be held at Harvard University covering a wide variety FOSS4G software and technology topics. Workshops will be held on Monday - Tuesday.
  • The JS.Geo conference will be held on Tuesday, Aug. 15 and we are providing a co-registration package with your FOSS4G registration. Co-registering will be your only opportunity to sign-up for the seat-limited JS.Geo event until Monday, July 17th, and will ensure you have a spot.
  • There will be a Business-to-Business session on Tuesday, Aug. 15th. Come see how a variety of businesses and sponsors are leveraging FOSS4G software while also giving back to the FOSS4G community.
